### Mock Strategy Applied

**ldoce5-api (`tests/test_ldoce5_api.py`)**
- `sys.modules` injection before `import main` — avoids needing real MDX binary files
- `app.state.mdx_builder`: `MagicMock()` with `.mdx_lookup()` returning raw HTML
- `app.state.mdd_wrapper`: `MagicMock()` (not exercised in P0)
- `app.state.llm_client`: `None` (AI selection not exercised in P0)
- Audio URL conversion: `.spx` → `.mp3` (matches `/lookup` response contract)

**anki-writer (`tests/test_anki_writer.py`)**
- `app.state.ldoce5_client`: `AsyncMock()` (injected directly into `app.state`)
- `anki_writer._add_note_and_sync`: patched per-test via `unittest.mock.patch`
  (avoids importing `anki` package, not available in dev/CI)
- **Critical fix**: `httpx.Response(200, json=...)` causes `RuntimeError` in `raise_for_status()`
  when `request` attribute is `None` (httpx ≥0.24 behaviour). Fixed by using `MagicMock()`
  with `.raise_for_status = MagicMock()` and `.json.return_value = dict` instead.
